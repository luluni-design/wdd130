Aloha and welcome to the Oahu! The warm days, peaceful nights and plumeria-scented breezes make Hawaii one of the most pleasant places in the world to visit.Sometimes called “The Gathering Place,” Oahu certainly lives up to its name. The third largest Hawaiian island is home to the majority of Hawaii's diverse population, a fusion of East and West cultures rooted in the values and traditions of the Native Hawaiian people.

It's this fundamental contrast between the ancient and the modern that makes discovering Oahu — from bustling city life to laidback surf towns — so enjoyable. You'll find plenty of different options for exploring Oahu's different regions, the island's many activities, its eclectic restaurants and accommodations.Experience Hawaii through the eyes of a local private guide! Browse our tour 'suggestions' - each can be customized just for you. Discover the island's hidden gems, local stories, natural wonders and cultural hotspots on a private Hawaii tour.
